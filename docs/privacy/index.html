Privacy

Privacy policy

This page explains, in plain language, the data processed when you browse this portfolio or use its contact form.

Controller and purposes

Guillaume Sabiron is responsible for this website. Data is used only to reply to messages sent through the contact form, measure site audience and performance in aggregate, and count CV downloads in aggregate.

Contact form

The form sends your name, email address and message to Formspree. This information is used solely to reply to your request and is retained only for the time needed to manage that exchange, unless a legal obligation requires otherwise. Please do not send sensitive information through this form.

Audience measurement and CV downloads

The website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate traffic and performance. CV downloads are counted through a Cloudflare Worker, which records only an aggregated event: CV language and date, with no visitor account or advertising profile.

Cookies, third-party content and external links

The website does not use targeted advertising. YouTube videos are loaded only after an explicit click on a video button. The interactive map and external links may then be governed by their own privacy policies. If the use of trackers changes, this page will be updated and, where required, a consent mechanism will be offered before they are activated.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction or deletion of your data, or ask any question about its processing, using the contact form. You may also lodge a complaint with the relevant data-protection authority.
